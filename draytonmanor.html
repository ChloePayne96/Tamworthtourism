Drayton Manor

Drayton Manor theme park is a must visit, when you visit Tamworth. The Theme park has a Thomas the tank engine land, for children. As well as its own zoo. That houses a variety of animals including, Meerkat's, birds of prey, reptiles, and monkeys to name a few. Including animals that are classed as an endangered species. The Theme park has an assortment of rides and attractions from fairground like rides, such as carousels and dodgems to rollercoasters like G-force.The Theme park also has its own 4D cinema. There is also a hotel on site. Which includes a variety of themed rooms, including a Thomas the tank engine themed room The popular theme park also offers places to hire for Conferences and parties such as the Tower suite
